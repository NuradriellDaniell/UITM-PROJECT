Introduction

This project, titled “Explore Sumiran Eco Park” focuses on developing an interactive multimedia presentation that highlights the beauty and uniqueness of Sumiran Eco Park one of Sarawak’s most relaxing and eco-friendly destinations. The main goal of this project is to use digital technology to introduce the park in a creative and engaging way that attracts tourist and raises awareness about sustainable tourism in Malaysia. In this modern digital era, multimedia has become one of the most powerful tools for communication. By combining text, images, videos, sound, animation, and interactivity this project aims to bring the natural environment of Sumiran Eco Park to life in a virtual format. Users will be able to explore different areas of the park through photo slideshows, video clips and interactive quizzes that make learning more enjoyable. This project is not only designed to promote tourism but also to educate the audience about environmental protection and eco-friendly lifestyles. The presentation will emphasize the park’s peaceful atmosphere, recreational activities and community-based programs that support sustainability. It will serve as a digital guide that inspires people to connect with nature, plan outdoor activities and appreciate the importance of preserving the environment for future generations. By integrating creativity with technology, this multimedia project hopes to leave an impression on its users and encourage them to personally experience the wonders of Sumiran Eco Park in Batu Kawa.

Project Background

This interactive multimedia project titled “Explore Sumiran Eco Park” is designed to promote one of Sarawak’s most popular eco-tourism attractions, Sumiran Eco Park located in Batu Kawa. The project combines several multimedia elements such as video, audio, text, animation and graphics to create an engaging and educational experience for users. Each element plays a key role in conveying information, enhancing user interaction and promoting environmental awareness.

  • Video: Video content is used to introduce the beauty and activities available at Sumiran Eco Park. Short video clips will showcase the park’s peaceful environment, kayaking sessions, camping areas and team building events. These visuals help tell the story of how the park promotes both recreation and sustainability. The videos aim to attract viewers by giving them realistic experience of being at the park.
  • Audio: Sound is used to create an immersive experience throughout the presentation. Soft background music, combined with natural sounds such as birds chirping and water flowing helps set a relaxing and peaceful mood. Voice narration will also be included to guide users through the different sections of the project, explaining the park’s attractions and eco-friendly values clearly
  • Text: Written content supports the visuals by providing clear explanations and detailed information about Sumiran Eco Park. The text will describe the park’s background available facilities and eco-tourism activities. It also highlights the importance of environmental education, ensuring that users can understand the message even without relying solely on visuals or sounds
  • Animation: Animations are used to make the presentation more dynamic and interactive. For example, clickable icons and animated transitions are included to make navigation smoother and more enjoyable. These animations help users stay engaged while exploring the different sections of the project.
  • Graphics: Graphics such as image, charts, icons and infographics are included too visually present the park’s layout, activities and facilities. Infographics will summarize key information like ticket prices, opening hours and safety tips in a more visual and easier to read format.

Literature Review

In developing this interactive multimedia project, several existing tourism videos and multimedia presentations were analysed as references to understand how visual storytelling, audio design and interactivity can effectively promote eco-tourism destinations. These examples offer valuable guidance in designing the “Explore Sumiran Eco Park: Nature, Fun & Adventure” project to ensure it delivers information in an attractive, informative, and interactive way. One of the most relevant references is the “Malaysia Truly Asia” tourism campaign video by Tourism Malaysia. This video successfully combines cinematic visuals, background music, and cultural storytelling to create emotional appeal and national identity. It shows how music and narration can work together to highlight Malaysia’s natural beauty and cultural diversity. From this example, the project team learned the importance of using smooth transitions, strong visual rhythm, and warm narration to build a positive impression of Sumiran Eco Park. Another useful reference is the “Sarawak: More to Discover” promotional video, which focuses on eco-adventures and community experiences. The video features short yet impactful clips of waterfalls, rainforests, and local traditions, all supported by light background music and minimal text. This approach inspired the use of short, focused video segments in the Sumiran Eco Park project to keep viewers engaged without overwhelming them with information. Additionally, several YouTube travel vlogs filmed at eco-parks and nature resorts in Malaysia, such as Kubah National Park and Borneo Highlands, provided insight into how video creators use personal storytelling, drone shots, and ambient sounds to create an authentic visitor experience. These vlogs emphasize natural soundscapes and real-life moments, which influenced the decision to include natural ambient audio (birds, water flow, wind) in the project to enhance realism.By studying these examples, the development team gained a deeper understanding of how multimedia elements especially video and audio can work together to convey emotion, promote eco-friendly tourism, and engage different audiences. The techniques seen in these existing materials will serve as a creative and technical guideline throughout the production of the interactive multimedia project, ensuring that “Explore Sumiran Eco Park” reflects both professional quality and environmental storytelling.
